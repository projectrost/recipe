Tomato Souce Recipe

Making the perfect tomato requires three things:

    1. Top quality ingredients

    2. Care and attention to detail

    3. Endless patience

    Tomato Souce Ingredients

  • ½ gallon water
  • 1lb Italian sausage
  • 1 large onion (finely chopped)
  • cloves minced garlic
  • 1 tin of crushed plum tomatoes
  • 1 cup of tomato purée
  • 1¾ tablespoons basil
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • A big splash of olive oil
  • A pinch of dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper

Cooking Directions:

Heat the olive oil in a large sauce pot. Fry the garlic in the olive oil, then drop in the chopped onions, Continue to fry until the onions are soft and have started to color. Add the Italian sausage in the same pot an brown sausage on both sides then remove ½ the sausage and reserve. Pour ½ a gallon of boil in water over the remaining half of the sausages and boil for 10-12 minutes until done; remove and reserve. Repeat this process for the second half of the sausages (in reserve). Add tin of tomatoes, tomato purée, garlic, the dried oregano and the sugar. Place in a cooker for approx. 45mins to 1hr at 220° or Gas Mark 7, until most of the water has evaporated (about 3 cups left) and the olive oil starts to appear around the edge of the pan. Finish with salt and pepper to taste. Pour this delicious sauce over a pasta of your choice. Enjoy!!!
